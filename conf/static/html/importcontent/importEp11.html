No licence available

You only need a licence to import textiles and clothing if the items are being sent from Belarus or North Korea.

It's not possible for this service to say for certain that you do not need a licence - it is still your responsibility to make sure.

If you still think you may need a licence, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
