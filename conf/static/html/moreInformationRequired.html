You will need some information before raising a query. In order to raise a control list classification query for your goods please log on to SPIRE.

Warning: Always double-check if your goods are controlled. If you export controlled goods without the correct export licence you could:
  • have your goods seized
  • face a fine
  • serve up to 10 years in prison

Before you raise a query

Get a technical expert who understands your good's design and technical specifications.

If you don't have a technical expert to help you then read the following guidance on the GOV.UK website:

  • Export military or dual use goods, services or technology: special rules
  • Do I need an export licence?
  • UK Strategic Export Control Lists
