You may need an import licence


You need a licence to import certain textiles and clothing made in Belarus. You need a licence regardless of how the goods are ordered or shipped, unless they are already in free circulation either in the EU or Turkey prior to their transfer to the UK.

Read about restrictions on imports of textiles and clothing from Belarus.

You can apply for an import licence using the Import Case Management System (ICMS). Log in to or register for ICMS.

Read about how to apply for a textiles licence using ICMS.

You can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
