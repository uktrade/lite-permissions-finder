You may not need a licence, but you must follow hazardous substance guidelines


You must make sure you identify all potential risks to human health and the environment presented by your goods. This is called self-classification and is administered by the European Chemicals Agency. Read how to classify your goods.

You must also make sure they are correctly labelled. Read how to correctly label chemicals.

If you import more than one tonne of chemicals per year, you must also register with the European Chemicals Agency.
