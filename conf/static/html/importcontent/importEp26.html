You need a CITES permit


You must have a CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species) permit to import endangered animals.

Apply for a CITES permit, or read how to complete a CITES application form. Read more about CITES.

You need a licence to import any non-native animal to the UK - apply using form RM01. Read guidance on importing non-native animals.

Non-native animals may also be covered by the Balai Directive. Read about the requirements of the Directive and see a list of what counts as a non-native species.

Not all animals are allowed to be imported. Use Species+ to find out the rules for a particular species.
