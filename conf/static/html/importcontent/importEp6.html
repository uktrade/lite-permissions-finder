Contact the Import Licensing Branch


Many goods are not allowed to be imported into the EU from Syria, including:

  • weapons and other military equipment
  • oil and petroleum products
  • gold and precious metals
  • diamonds

Read about sanctions on international trade with Syria.

You should contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333 to check whether your items are allowed to be imported from Syria.
