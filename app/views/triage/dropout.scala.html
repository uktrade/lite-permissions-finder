You have not found your item in the military list. This does not mean that your item is not controlled.

Warning: Always double-check if your items are controlled. You face up to 10 years in prison, fines and your items may be seized if you export controlled items without the correct licence.

What you should do next

  1. Conduct your search with a technical officer and any relevant item design margins.
  2. Go back to clarify the type of item (LINK TO BE UPDATED) and which category it falls under within the Military List
  3. Consider whether your item is specially designed for military use or not (LINK TO BE UPDATED). If your item is not military than you should consider the dual use list (LINK TO BE UPDATED).

Export control licenses

You cannot export items without a licence if:

  • you know or suspect they'll be used in a weapons of mass destruction programme or delivery system (also known as 'end-use controls')
  • you're exporting dual-use items that do not usually need an export licence but you know or suspect they might be incorporated into military equipment for use in embargoed countries
  • there are embargoes, sanctions or other restrictions which cover your export destination

Find out more about end-use sanctions and embargoes
