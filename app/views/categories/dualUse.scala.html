Some things exported for civilian purposes can also have a military use and therefore need a licence. They are typically high-tech, advanced products, often with military standard specifications. These are called dual-use items.

Many items you might not think need a licence may in fact have a dual use, such as:

  • protective suits, gloves and shoes
  • machine tools
  • computers
  • capacitors
  • microchips
  • telecommunications equipment
  • underwater equipment

There are many other examples of dual-use items, and not all of the items listed above will require a licence. Watch a short video about dual-use items: [video]
