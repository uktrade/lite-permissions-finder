No licence available

You cannot use this service to get an import licence. This is because either:

  • you do not need a licence to import your items, or
  • you have not been able to identify the correct item in this service

It's not possible to say for certain that you do not need a licence - it is still your responsibility to make sure.

If you still think you may need a licence, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@trade.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
