You may not be allowed to import your items


You are not allowed to import anti-personnel mines and their components. Read about equipment that is banned under anti-personnel mine legislation.

You can import certain goods if they are solely for purposes of training in anti-mine practices such as detection, clearance or destruction. If you think this may apply to your items, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
