If you have arrived here by selecting Firearms or Software and Technology please log on to SPIRE and raise a control list classification query for your goods.

Otherwise based on the choice you've made, you need to find out more about your goods.

Warning: Always double-check if your goods are controlled. If you export controlled goods without the correct export licence you could:
  • have your goods seized
  • face a fine
  • serve up to 10 years in prison

What you should do next

  1. Get a technical expert who understands your good's design and technical specifications to check if your item:

    • is specially designed or modified for a military application (also known as military use)
    • is defined as a component, software or technology
    • needs to be checked against another one of the UK Strategic Export Control Lists

  2. If you don't have a technical expert to help you then read the following guidance on the GOV.UK website:

    • Export military or dual use goods, services or technology: special rules
    • Do I need an export licence?
    • UK Strategic Export Control Lists
