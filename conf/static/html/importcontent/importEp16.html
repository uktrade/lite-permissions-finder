You may need a licence from the Office for Nuclear Regulation


You need a licence to import the following materials from outside the EU:

  • plutonium
  • uranium ore concentrates
  • uranium 233
  • uranium enriched in the isotopes 233 or 235
  • natural uranium and mixtures
  • compounds and alloys containing any of the above, including spent or irradiated nuclear reactor fuel elements (cartridges)

The Office for Nuclear Regulation is responsible for the import licensing of restricted nuclear materials. Read guidance on how to apply for an import licence for nuclear materials.
