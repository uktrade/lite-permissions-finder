If you are exporting outside the EU, you need to have your goods inspected by the Food and Environment Research Agency (Fera) to make sure that the export of flowers, wildlife, plants and seeds meets the requirements of importing countries. Contact Fera to find your nearest plant health and seed inspector.

If you are exporting within the EU, you may require a plant passport. This depends on the type of items you want to export and the nature of your business. Read more about plant passports.
