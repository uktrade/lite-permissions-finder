Whether you need a licence will depend on where your goods are going. You will have to research the food safety regulations in the countries you choose to export food to.

You don't usually need a licence to export food products to another EU country. Contact the authority in the destination country, or their foreign embassy in the UK, to check if there are any specific restrictions.

If you are exporting food to non-EU destinations, you will have to check whether authorities there impose restrictions on the import of food. Check the Department for International Trade's country guides for specific information on many destinations.
