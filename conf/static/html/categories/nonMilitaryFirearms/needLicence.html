You need a standard individual export licence (SIEL) to sell, gift, loan or permanently transfer non-military firearms and related ammunition.

SIELs will be added to this service later, but for now you can apply for a SIEL through SPIRE.

Antique firearms

You may also need an Arts Council licence if your firearms are over 50 years old, depending on their value.

Read Arts Council guidance on export licensing for cultural goods..
