You can use the waste export controls service to find out which controls apply to you or if the waste you’re exporting is prohibited.

You cannot export:

  • hazardous or household waste
  • waste for disposal except in very special circumstances, for example if there’s an emergency or a trial for new technology

Notification controls

You need the consent of destination countries to export waste considered high risk. This consent is called a notification control.

Use the International Waste Shipments service to apply for a notification control.

Green-list waste

Green-list waste is usually considered a low risk to the environment. You can export green-list waste without permission in most cases. You must follow all import rules of the destination country and it is your responsibility to check all the requirements that apply there.

See the list of waste classed as green list.
